Job Description


Skill required: Governance - Data Governance
Designation: Management Level - Analyst
Job Location: Chennai
Qualifications: Any Graduation
Years of Experience: 3-5 years

What would you do?
The Business Reporting and Governance vertical helps to deploy and deliver robust tracking mechanism for SLA/KPI or any other operations on a day to day basis. The Governance team will be responsible for contractual compliance of various aspects of contract like Governance, Reporting, Incident Management, Change Management and Survey Management along with driving automation and analytics. In Data Governance, you will be assessing, managing, using, improving, monitoring, maintaining and protecting organizational information through a system of decision rights and accountabilities for information-related processes, executed according to agreed-upon models which describe who can take what actions, with what information, when, under what circumstances and using what methods.
What are we looking for?
We are looking for individuals who have the following skillset:
Data Maintenance
Adaptable and flexible
Ability to work well in a team
Written and verbal communication
It would be a value add if you are proficient in:
Change Tracking
Data Reporting
Know Your Customer (KYC) Operations
