✅ Emoluments:

✔️ Contract Medical Officer – Field Duty (FMO): ₹ 75,000/- per month
✔️ Contract Medical Officer – General Duty (GDMO) (Full Time): ₹ 72,000/- per month
✔️ Contract Medical Officer – Homeopathy: ₹ 55,000/- per month


✅ Educational Qualifications:

✔️ GDMO / FMO: Bachelor of Medicine and Bachelor of Surgery (MBBS).
✔️ Contract Medical Officer - Homeopathy: Bachelor of Homeopathic Medicine and Surgery (BHMS).
✅ Selection Process: On the basis of following marks:-
✔️ Qualification (70 Marks) ✔️ Interview (30 Marks)

✅ How to Apply: Eligible candidates with valid registration with Medical Council of India / State Medical Council may appear for a Walk in Interview as per Schedule:-

✔️ Interview Dates: 23/12/2020 and 24/12/2020
✔️ Reporting Time: 09:00 Hrs. to 11:00 Hrs.

✔️ Venue: ONGC Officer's Club, ONGC, Maharpura Road, Barod
